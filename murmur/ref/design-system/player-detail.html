The Lighthouse Keeper's Daughter

"Some secrets are worth keeping."

Duration 12 MIN
Paths 4
Voice Eleanor

The Blackwood Reach lighthouse has stood silent for a century, its lantern extinguished by a storm that claimed more than just the light. You arrive as the estranged granddaughter of its last keeper, carrying only a silver key and a memory of a lullaby.

As the tide recedes, the stones begin to whisper. Will you follow the echoes into the spiral staircase of the past, or let the waves wash away the truth of what your family left behind in the salt and spray?
